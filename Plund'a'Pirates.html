Plund'a'Pirates

A game made made by 2 people in 26h.

Overview

This my first ever attempt at a game jam, this one organised by my university, and made with @almfie. The game was supposed to fit, or otherwise incorporate, a "reveal". We have decided on making metal detecting cover that idea, which later evolved into pirates searching for treasure.

What have we got so far?

The game supports same-screen 2 player pvp, with funcionality for both keyboard and controllers. The aim of the game is to search the island you and a friend of yours are placed on, and find more treasure than your friend. As of now, the game is unfinished, and doesn't yet contain a win/loss state and support for more than 2 players, however we discussed the possibility of finishing this game up in the future, and have decided to put it on hold for now.
